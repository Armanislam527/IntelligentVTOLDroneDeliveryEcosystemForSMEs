Project Overview

GreenLift AI is an innovative VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) drone delivery ecosystem powered by solar energy and artificial intelligence, designed specifically to empower Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) across Bangladesh. Our solution integrates sustainable logistics with business intelligence to transform last-mile delivery operations.
